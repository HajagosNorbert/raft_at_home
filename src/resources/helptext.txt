Commands available:
'[direction-number]': move to a direction. Also denoted on the number pad:
   '1': down-left
   '2': down
   '3': down-right
   '4': left
   '5': current tile. (drink/eat/fish) if you are on a (purifier/fireplace/ocean) tile
   '6': right
   '7': up-left
   '8': up
   '9': up-right

'g [direction-number]': gather resource from the tile specified by the direction-number

'b [what] [direction-number]': builds the specified craftable on the tile specified by the direction-number.
[what] can be the following
   'spear' (arbitrary direction number)
   'fireplace'
   'purifier'
   'net'
   'platform'

And of course 'h' for help :)
Press [Enter] to continue.
